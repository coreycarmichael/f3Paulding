About F3

F3 is a free, peer-led workout group for men that meets outdoors in all weather conditions.

The Three F's

  • Fitness

    Free, outdoor workouts that are peer-led and open to all men.

  • Fellowship

    Building relationships and community through shared experiences.

  • Faith

    Growing in faith and character through physical and mental challenges.

Core Principles

  • Free of charge
  • Open to all men
  • Held outdoors
  • Peer-led
  • Ends with a Circle of Trust (COT)
